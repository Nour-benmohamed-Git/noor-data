Privacy Policy

Effective 14 June 2026

Noor is a Quran reading and recitation companion built to be private by design. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. This policy explains exactly what that means.

The short version: Noor has no accounts and no login. Your recitation audio is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, uploaded, or sent to any server. Your progress, bookmarks, and settings stay on your device. We use no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

1. Who we are

This application ("Noor", "the app", "we", "us") is published by Noor App. You can reach us at mediakit.contact@gmail.com for any privacy question.

2. Information we do not collect

Noor does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any other identifying information. We do not maintain user profiles and we have no servers that store information about you.

3. Recitation and your microphone

When you use the recitation features, Noor accesses your microphone only while that feature is active. The audio is analysed on your device by an offline speech-recognition model that ships inside the app. This processing happens entirely on your device:

  • Your voice is not recorded to a file.
  • Your voice is not uploaded to us or to any third party.
  • The recitation features work with no internet connection at all.

Microphone access is requested only when you start a recitation session and is used for nothing else.

4. Information stored on your device

To make the app useful, Noor saves certain information locally on your device only — for example your reading position, bookmarks, memorization progress, daily-goal and streak data, mistake counts, and your display preferences. This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is removed when you uninstall the app. You can also reset your saved progress and history from within the app under Settings → Data & Privacy.

5. Network requests

Noor downloads Quran text, translations, tafsir, and word-by-word data from public content-delivery networks (such as jsDelivr and GitHub), and reciter audio from a public Quran audio archive (everyayah.com), so the content can then be used offline. These are ordinary secure (HTTPS) requests. As with visiting any website, the content host may record your IP address in its own server logs as part of normal operation, but Noor does not attach your identity or any personal data to these requests, and we do not receive that information.

6. No analytics, tracking, or advertising

Noor contains no analytics services, no crash-reporting services, no advertising networks, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not build advertising profiles and we do not sell or share data, because we do not collect it.

7. Children's privacy

Noor is suitable for users of all ages. Because the app collects no personal data from anyone, it collects no personal data from children. We do not knowingly gather information from children, and there is no profile or account to create.

8. Your rights

Privacy laws such as the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) give you rights over your personal data, including access, correction, and erasure. Because Noor holds no personal data about you on any server, there is nothing for us to retrieve, change, or delete on your behalf. All data the app uses lives on your device and is under your control. If you have questions about your rights, contact us at mediakit.contact@gmail.com.

9. Data retention

We retain no personal data. Data stored locally by the app persists on your device until you clear it in-app or uninstall the app.

10. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the page and revise the effective date above. Material changes will be reflected in the app's About screen.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to mediakit.contact@gmail.com.
